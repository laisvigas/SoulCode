The Nintendo Switch is Finally Here!

Introducing the Nintendo Switch, the revolutionary new gaming system from Nintendo that redefines how and where you can play! The Switch seamlessly transitions between a powerful home console and a portable handheld device, letting you take your favorite games with you, wherever you go. With a vibrant 6.2-inch touchscreen display and innovative Joy-Con controllers, you can play solo or with friends in ways never before possible. Slide the Joy-Cons onto the console to play on the go, or attach them to the included grip for a classic controller feel when docked and playing on your TV. Feature Description
Design Hybrid console that functions as both a home console and portable handheld device

Storage 32GB internal storage, expandable with microSD card
Wi-Fi Connectivity Supports online play, game downloads, and access to Nintendo eShop
Multiplayer Options Local multiplayer with Joy-Cons and online multiplayer via Nintendo Switch Online
Resolution 1080p in docked mode, 720p in handheld mode
Game Library Wide selection including exclusives like The Legend of Zelda: Breath of the Wild and Mario Kart
